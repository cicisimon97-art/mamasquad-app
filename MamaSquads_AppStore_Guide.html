Description:

Less Chaos. More Playdates.

We saw a need for moms to build villages around them, and we found a solution to make it happen. MamaSquads is a verified, moms-only community where you can find your people, organize playdates, and build the village every mom deserves.

Create or join local mom squads, plan playdates and events, and connect with verified moms in your area - all in one place. No strangers, no guesswork, just real moms supporting each other.

Features:

- Verified, moms-only community
- Create and join local mom groups
- Plan and RSVP to playdates and events
- Group chat and coordination
- Simple, intuitive design
